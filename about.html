About rest

Our Story

Founded in 2015, rest began as a small team of administrative professionals with a shared vision: to empower businesses by streamlining their operations and freeing them to focus on growth. We recognized that many companies, especially startups and small businesses, struggled with the burden of administrative tasks. Our mission was to provide expert, reliable, and cost-effective administrative support, tailored to each client's unique needs.

Over the years, we've grown from a local service provider to a trusted partner for businesses across the region. We've expanded our service offerings to include virtual assistance, bookkeeping, HR support, and project management. Our commitment to excellence and client satisfaction has earned us a reputation as a leading administrative services provider.
